Course Overview

Welcome to the Certified Ethical Hacking course - your comprehensive journey into the world of cybersecurity and ethical penetration testing. This course is designed to teach you the skills and techniques used by malicious hackers, but from a defensive perspective. Learn to think like an attacker to better defend your organization's critical assets.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: All techniques taught in this course are for educational and defensive purposes only. Use these skills responsibly and only on systems you own or have explicit permission to test.
